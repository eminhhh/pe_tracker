Problem 991: Fruit Salad
Source: https://projecteuler.net/minimal=991

$$\frac {\unicode{x1F34E}} {\unicode{x1F34C} + \unicode{x1F34D}} + \frac {\unicode{x1F34C}} {\unicode{x1F34D} + \unicode{x1F34E}} + \frac {\unicode{x1F34D}} {\unicode{x1F34E} + \unicode{x1F34D}} = 4$$
and $\unicode{x1F34E} + \unicode{x1F34C} + \unicode{x1F34D} \le 10^7$.
